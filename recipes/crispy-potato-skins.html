Crispy Potato Skins

Desciption

Stop food waste with these incredible crispy potato skins! Whether you have leftover potato skins from making mash, boiling potatoes or you just want to peel some potatoes so that you can make this great simple snack; do your part and don't throw any of those tasty potato skins away. Use this easy recipe to make a great snack or side dish.

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes or potato skins
  • Oil
  • Flour
  • Seasonings- you do not need to use the exact same as we do, however we will say what we have used in the steps below. Use your favourite, or whatever you have available!

Steps:

  1. Skip this step if you have already peeled your potatoes. Peel your potatoes, keep the skins to one side and put the rest of the potato away as we don't need that for this recipe. (If you're stuck with what to make with these left over potatoes, check out our delicious Butter Potato Curry recipe!
  2. Put in a bowl with some oil and your chosen seasonings. We used: salt, pepper, cajun seasoning, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano & tumeric.
  3. Mix the potato skins with the oil and seasonings, until they're all evenly spread.
  4. Add some flour to the bowl if you want your potato skins to be extra crunchy!
  5. Put in an air-fryer or oven at 200 degrees celcius for 18-25 minutes- depending on how think your peels are and how crispy you want them.
  6. Shake the peels every 6-8 minutes until golden brown.
  7. Enjoy your tasty crispy potato skins!
